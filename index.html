Indexterous.

Indexterous is a project in three parts.

  • Correlation engine
    • Given two sets of numbers, both in order,
  • News scraper
    • Identify popular stories (perhaps threshold of 100 articles)
    • For each story, download text from each article
    • Store each article in database table for 3 years
    • Optional: Cluster words and guess
    • Optional: Translate languages
  • Lingustic Interpreter
    • Predicting the Semantic Orientation of Adjectives
    • For each article, analyze text and build an index from several dimensions:
      • Positive/Negative
      • Important/Unimportant
      • Unusual/Normal

What is the goal?

I'm trying to develop a good way of correlating the mood of news reports to individual stocks
